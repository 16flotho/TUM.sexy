TUM Calendar Proxy

About

Nice and easy proxy to remove some clutter from the TUM online iCal export. E.g.: replaces 'Tutorübung' with 'T'

HowTo

  1. Grab the URL from the TUMO calendar
  2. Copy 'n' Paste the query string (everything after the ? sign) and append it to this url
  3. Go to Google Calendar (or similar) and import the resulting url
